Airport Management System

The website caters to airport management needs and offers several features, including a user view that displays arrival and departure information. It also includes an employee login for both airline and airport staff, as well as admin management and baggage handling capabilities. The user view provides details on incoming and outgoing flights, including their respective gate numbers. Employees can access their designated areas through the login portal, where airline personnel can add flights and airport staff can manage gates. The admin function enables the management of employee accounts and the addition of new personnel. The system automatically assigns gate numbers to flights and efficiently manages baggage carousel assignments. This website is hosted on the AWS Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2) platform and includes an autoscaling load balancer to handle high traffic demands.
